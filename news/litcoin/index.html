LASIGE-Unicage team scored 7th in NASA Litcoin NLP Competition

April 19, 2022

2022

I’m pleased to announce that the Lasige-Unicage team achieved the 7th place in the LitCoin Challenge and was awarded a cash prize of 5.000 USD!

Check out the winners of the LitCoin Natural Language Processing (#NLP) Challenge! Participants created a model that could help scientists make connections between research concepts. This can increase the use of data in solving health challenges: https://t.co/57zmO5szcL pic.twitter.com/ejOXi8BABu

— NCATS (@ncats_nih_gov) April 18, 2022

The LitCoin Challenge was part of the NASA Tournament Lab and was hosted by NCBI (The National Center for Biotechnology Information), NCATS (The National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences) and NIH (National Institutes of Health).

There were two phases with different goals:

  • Phase 1: Identification of biomedical entities in abstracts of scientific texts
  • Phase 2: Identification of the relations between the biomedical entities and prediction of novelty

I was specifically responsible for the approach developed to tackle the first phase, which consisted of a Named Entity Recognition model able to identify entities in abstracts of the following types:

  • DiseaseOrPhenotypicFeature
  • ChemicalEntity
  • OrganismTaxon
  • GeneOrGeneProduct
  • SequenceVariant
  • CellLine
